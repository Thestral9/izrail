With additional skills in Python, C++, Java, and basic knowledge of Django, React, and Node.js, Izrail is a well-rounded engineer. He combines his deep technical knowledge with a problem-solving mindset, consistently delivering high-impact solutions in fast-paced, dynamic environments.
